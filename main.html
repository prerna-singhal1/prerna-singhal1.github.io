Who I Am

Engineer.
Researcher.
Builder.

I'm a student at Empros International School — serving as Head Girl and President of the CS Club. I build AI systems that actually ship to real users: multilingual voice companions for elderly care, end-to-end healthcare networks, and agentic tools that automate complex workflows.

My technical obsession spans agentic AI architectures, memory systems, model fine-tuning, and project frameworks. I've been recognized by AIIMS Delhi (India's #1 medical institute) for my work in healthcare AI, and shortlisted as one of India's top 50 students by ISRO.

I write research papers, publish books, win international olympiads, and run a non-profit — often simultaneously. I believe the best builders aren't just technically strong; they understand the humans their systems serve.

Viksit Youth — Student-Led Non-Profit

I founded Viksit Youth — a student-driven non-profit focused on environmental awareness, civic education, and elder care. VIKSIT stands for the younger generation advancing toward responsible and aware citizenship.

We've organized large-scale cleanup drives, provided sustained support to senior citizens in old age homes, and delivered foundational civic and science sessions at government schools across the region.

Every initiative is student-led, with short-term measurable goals designed for environmental good and community uplift. We believe the best technology is useless if we don't also show up for our communities in person.
